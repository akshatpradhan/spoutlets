  • Sometimes I draw silly pictures of what my anxiety looks like and make fun of the drawing. What are some things you do to help with your anxiety?

    shared by tarotblades
    5 comments 10 views

    Things that help um, CBT therapy, and writing down how you feel and what you are thinking can really help you to calm down and be more logical. Distraction, playing video games, Sudoku, anything that get's you to focus outside of yourself. Talking to others who have anxiety, really helps you not to feel so crazy :) Deep breathing, meditation, learning how to relax. Drinking water or hot tea or chewing gum. Cleaning can help too as you're physically doing something so burning off the excess anxiety and it's a good distraction. Positive thinking. Taking random pictures and looking back on them helps when you're feeling down or like nothings going to improve. I think looking at happy pictures triggers off good memories and feelings. Cut down on coffee and alcohol. Eat well, take vitamins, omega 3. Don't be hard on yourself. That's all I can think of right now :)
